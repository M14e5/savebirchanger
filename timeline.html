INSPECTOR'S REPORT & MINISTERIAL DECISION

Upcoming

  • Guarantees our evidence is heard and tested - not a particular outcome

🎯 KEY TAKEAWAY: ONE HURDLE WON, THE NEXT FIGHT IS AT INQUIRY

On 10th June 2026 the planning committee unanimously refused both applications - but on 1st July 2026 the Secretary of State called them in, so the council will never issue that refusal. The whole case now goes to a public inquiry. This guarantees our evidence is heard and properly tested in public; it does not guarantee the outcome. We've won the first battle, but not the war.
