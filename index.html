Understanding the Developer's Strategy

The "Grey Belt" Loophole

City & Country is exploiting new government policy on "grey belt" land to build on our Green Belt. They claim the land doesn't serve Green Belt purposes, but it clearly:

  • Prevents the merger of Birchanger, Stansted, and Bishop's Stortford
  • Safeguards countryside from encroachment
  • Preserves the setting of historic communities
  • Maintains the individual character of each settlement

Don't be fooled: This is prime agricultural Green Belt land, not degraded "grey belt" as the developer suggests.
