Town Under Pressure: 2,000+ Homes With No Infrastructure Plan

City & Country wants to expand Stansted dramatically towards Birchanger and Bishop's Stortford, overwhelming our already strained infrastructure. With airport traffic, school places at capacity, and GP surgeries overloaded, this development would push our town beyond breaking point. Stansted residents must act now!

Why Stansted's Boundaries Matter

Stansted Mountfitchet is a historic market town with a distinct identity, not a suburb of Bishop's Stortford or an airport dormitory. Our town has grown sustainably over centuries - this mega-development threatens to destroy that balance overnight.

Infrastructure Already at Breaking Point

0 New Secondary School Places
45min+ Current GP Wait Times
Daily Traffic Gridlock at Peak Times
140% Primary Schools Over Capacity

Unlike developers' promises, the reality for Stansted residents would be:

  • Schools Crisis: Forest Hall School and our primary schools are already oversubscribed. Where will 3,000+ new children go to school?
  • Medical Emergency: Lower Street Surgery and Church Road Surgery struggle to meet current demand. Adding thousands more patients without new facilities is a disaster waiting to happen.
  • Traffic Nightmare: Grove Hill, Cambridge Road, and Church Road already gridlock at peak times. Add airport traffic and this development - total paralysis.
  • Parking Chaos: Our town center and station already suffer from airport parking overspill. More residents means nowhere for anyone to park.
  • Loss of Town Identity: We'd become a continuous urban sprawl from Bishop's Stortford through Stansted to Birchanger - three distinct places merged into one.
  • Retail & Services Overload: Lower Street shops, banks, and services can't cope with doubling the customer base overnight.
